Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun. It is known as the "Red Planet" due to the iron-rich soil. Mars has a thin atmosphere, mostly made of carbon dioxide. It features polar ice caps, canyons, volcanoes, and signs of past water. Temperatures are low, and conditions are harsh for life.
